Computer Science and Medical Engineering (CSME)

The Computer Science and Medical Engineering (CSME) program integrates core principles of computer science with medical engineering applications. This program aims to prepare students for careers where technology and healthcare intersect. Students will learn about medical imaging, bioinformatics, health informatics, and the development of medical software systems. The curriculum is designed to equip students with both technical skills and domain knowledge to address healthcare challenges using computing technologies.
